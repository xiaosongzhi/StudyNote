# Lesson 02

# 🍽️ Restaurant Ordering

> **Chapter 1 · Starting a New Life**

Version: 1.0

Estimated Time: 60–90 mins

---

# 📖 Story

Yesterday, you finished your first shopping trip.

Today, you don't feel like cooking.

You decide to have dinner at a nearby restaurant.

When you walk in, a waiter **greets** you with a smile.  //greet  --verb  问候，迎接，欢迎

Can you order your first meal in English?

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

After this lesson, you will be able to:

- Ask for a table.
- Order food and drinks.
- Ask for **recommendations**.    //有什么推荐的
- Ask about the menu.
- Pay the bill.
- Finish a complete restaurant conversation.

---

# 🧊 Warm-up

Answer these questions.

1. How often do you eat at restaurants?

2. What is your favorite food?

3. Do you like Chinese food or Western food?

# 💬 Useful Expressions

## Entering the Restaurant

How many people?

Just one, please.

A table for two, please.

Do you have a reservation?

No, I don't.

---

## Ordering Food

May I have the menu, please?

I'd like...

I'll have...

I'd like to order...

What do you recommend?

---

## Ordering Drinks

I'd like a glass of water.

Could I have a Coke?

Coffee, please.

No ice, please.

---

## During the Meal

This is delicious.

Could I have some more water?

Excuse me.

---

## Paying

Could I have the bill, please?

Can I pay by card?

Can we split the bill?

Keep the change.

---

# ⭐ Native Expressions

Instead of saying

❌ Give me a steak.

Say

✅ I'd like a steak, please.

---

Instead of saying

❌ Menu!

Say

✅ May I have the menu, please?

---

Instead of saying

❌ I want water.

Say

✅ I'd like a glass of water.

---

# 🎭 Dialogue

## Scene 1

**You walk into the restaurant.**

Waiter:

Good evening!

Welcome!

How many people?

You:

Just one, please.

---

## Scene 2

Waiter:

Here is the menu.

You:

Thank you.

I'd like a steak, please.

Waiter:

How would you like it cooked?

You:

Medium, please.

---

## Scene 3

Waiter:

Anything to drink?

You:

I'd like a glass of orange juice.

---

## Scene 4

After the meal

You:

Could I have the bill, please?

Waiter:

Certainly.

Here you are.

You:

Can I pay by card?

Waiter:

Of course.
