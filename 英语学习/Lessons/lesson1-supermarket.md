# Lesson 01

# 🛒 Supermarket Shopping

> **Chapter 1 · Starting a New Life**

Version: 1.0

Estimated Time: 60–90 mins

---

# 📖 Story

You have just moved into your new apartment.

There is almost nothing inside.

Today is your first day in a new city.

Your refrigerator is empty.

You need to buy food and daily necessities.

You walk into a large supermarket for the first time.

Everything is written in English.

Can you finish your first shopping trip?

**This is where your journey begins.**

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

After this lesson, you will be able to:

- Ask where products are.
- Ask for help politely.
- Buy food and daily necessities.
- Ask about prices.
- Pay by card or cash.
- Finish a complete supermarket conversation.

---

# 🧊 Warm-up

Answer these questions.

1. How often do you go to the supermarket?

2. What do you usually buy?

3. Do you prefer paying by card or cash?

# 💬 Useful Expressions

## Asking for help

Excuse me.

Could you help me?

I'm looking for...

Where can I find...?

Could you tell me where the ... is?

---

## Asking about prices

How much is this?

How much are these?

Is this on sale?

Is there any discount?

---

## Buying something

I'd like some bread.

I'd like a bottle of milk.

I'll take this one.

I'll take two.

---

## Paying

Can I pay by card?

Can I pay in cash?

Could I have a receipt, please?

Do you have a membership card?

---

# ⭐ Native Expressions

Instead of saying

❌ Give me milk.

Say

✅ I'd like some milk, please.

---

Instead of

❌ Where is milk?

Say

✅ Where can I find the milk?

---

Instead of

❌ Can pay by card?

Say

✅ Can I pay by card?

---

# 🎭 Dialogue

## Scene 1

**You enter the supermarket.**

Staff:

Hello!

Can I help you?

You:

Hi.

I'm looking for some milk.

Staff:

Sure.

It's in aisle five.

You:

Thank you.

---

## Scene 2

You:

I'd like some bread.

Staff:

Fresh bread is over there.

You:

Thank you.

I'll take one loaf.

---

## Scene 3

You:

Excuse me.

Where can I find the eggs?

Staff:

They're in aisle three.

You:

Thank you.

I'll go have a look.

---

## Scene 4

You find the eggs.

You:

Great.

I'll take one dozen.

---

## Scene 5

Checkout

Cashier:

Hello.

Did you find everything you needed?

You:

Yes.

Thank you.

Cashier:

Would you like a bag?

You:

Yes, please.

Cashier:

That'll be $26.80.

You:

Can I pay by card?

Cashier:

Of course.

Please tap your card.

You:

Thank you.

Cashier:

Here's your receipt.

Have a nice day!

You:

Thank you.

Have a nice day!

# ❌ Common Mistakes

❌ Can pay by card?

✅ Can I pay by card?

---

❌ Where is milk?

✅ Where can I find the milk?

---

❌ Give me bread.

✅ I'd like some bread, please.
