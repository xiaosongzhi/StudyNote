# Course Story

Welcome to **English Journey**.

This is not just an English course.

It is a journey.

In this journey, **you are the main character**.

You have just arrived in a new English-speaking city.

Everything is new.

You need to build a new life here.

Every lesson is a new challenge.

Every conversation moves your story forward.

By the end of the course, you will not only speak English more confidently, but also complete the journey from surviving in a new city to living and working there independently.
